Lazy little brother 働かない弟 Lazy little brother

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Mlungisi Dlamini

Darshan Soni

English

Level 1

I wake up and make fire.

僕は目を覚まして火をおこす。

I wake up and make fire.

I boil some water.

お湯を沸かす。

I boil some water.

I chop the firewood.

薪を割る。

I chop the firewood.

I stir the pot.

鍋をかき混ぜる。

I stir the pot.

I sweep the floor.

床を掃除する。

I sweep the floor.

I wash the dishes.

お皿を洗う。

I wash the dishes.

Why do I work so hard... ... when my brother is busy playing?

何で僕は一生懸命働くんだろう。弟は遊びに夢中だというのに。

Why do I work so hard... ... when my brother is busy playing?

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Mlungisi Dlamini
Read by: Darshan Soni
Language: English
Level 1
