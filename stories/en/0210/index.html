Tingi and the Cows ティンギと牛たち Tingi and the Cows

Ingrid Schechter

Ingrid Schechter

Darshan Soni

English

Level 2

Tingi lived with his grandmother.

ティンギはおばあさんと一緒に暮らしていました。

Tingi lived with his grandmother.

He used to look after the cows with her.

おばあさんと一緒に牛たちの世話をしていました。

He used to look after the cows with her.

One day the soldiers came.

ある日、兵士たちがやって来ました。

One day the soldiers came.

They took the cows away.

兵士たちは牛たちを連れて行ってしまいました。

They took the cows away.

Tingi and his grandmother ran away and hid.

ティンギとおばあさんは走って逃げました。

Tingi and his grandmother ran away and hid.

They hid in the bush until night.

二人は夜まで森に隠れていました。

They hid in the bush until night.

Then the soldiers came back.

やがて兵士たちが戻ってきました。

Then the soldiers came back.

Grandmother hid Tingi under the leaves.

おばあさんはティンギを木の葉の下に隠しました。

Grandmother hid Tingi under the leaves.

One of the soldiers put his foot right on him, but he kept quiet.

一人の兵士がティンギを踏みましたがティンギはじっとしていました。

One of the soldiers put his foot right on him, but he kept quiet.

When it was safe, Tingi and his grandmother came out.

静かになるとティンギとおばあさんは森から出てきました。

When it was safe, Tingi and his grandmother came out.

They crept home very quietly.

二人は音をたてないようそっと家に戻って行きました。

They crept home very quietly.

Written by: Ingrid Schechter
Illustrated by: Ingrid Schechter
Read by: Darshan Soni
Language: English
Level 2
